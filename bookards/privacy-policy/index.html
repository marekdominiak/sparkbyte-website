2. Children’s privacy

Bookards is designed for young children and families.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children, such as:

  • Child’s name
  • Email address
  • Home address
  • Phone number
  • Precise location
  • Photos
  • Contacts
  • Messages
  • Documents

Bookards does not include chat, social networking, public profiles, user-generated content, ads, analytics, crash reporting, or account login.

3. Information we collect

Bookards does not require users to create an account.

We do not collect personal information directly from users.

The app may store basic app preferences or progress locally on the device, for example which content has been unlocked or viewed. This information stays on the device and is not used to identify the child.

4. Premium purchase

Bookards may offer a one-time premium purchase to unlock additional content.

Payments are processed securely by Google Play Billing. We do not receive, collect, or store payment card details.

Google may process payment-related information according to Google’s own privacy policy and payment terms.

5. Ads

Bookards does not show third-party advertisements.

6. Analytics and tracking

Bookards does not use Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, advertising tracking, or third-party tracking tools.

Bookards does not use third-party advertising identifiers to track children across apps or websites.

If we add analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or account features in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and the app’s Google Play Data Safety information.

7. Internet and external links

Bookards may require internet access for app store functions such as premium purchase verification.

Any external links, such as links to our Privacy Policy or support contact, are intended for parents or guardians.

8. Data sharing

We do not sell personal data.

We do not share children’s personal information with advertisers, analytics providers, or data brokers.

Limited information may be processed by Google Play when users download the app or make a purchase, according to Google’s own policies.

9. Data security

We aim to keep the app simple and avoid collecting personal data wherever possible.

Because Bookards does not require accounts and does not directly collect personal information from children, the privacy risk is limited.

10. Data deletion

Bookards does not provide user accounts.

If the app stores local preferences or progress on the device, users can delete this information by uninstalling the app or clearing the app data from the device settings.

For any privacy or data deletion questions, please contact us at bookards@sparkbyte.eu.

11. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

If we make important changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page.
